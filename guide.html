How to Pay with CashApp

Follow these simple steps to pay the Lightning invoice using CashApp on your mobile device.

1

Open CashApp and Click the $ Icon

Open the CashApp on your mobile device. You'll see the main screen with a keypad showing "$0".

2

Scan the QR Code

Click the scan QR button in the top left corner to scan the QR code displayed on https://so-big-lightning-payment.vercel.app/

3

Select Cash as Your Funding Source

After scanning, CashApp will ask: "How do you want to fund your $1.25 bitcoin payment?"

You have two options:

  • Bitcoin: Use your existing Bitcoin balance (shown as "$X.XX available")
  • Cash: Use your Cash balance (shown as "$X.XX balance")

Select Cash by tapping on it, then tap "Continue".

4

Review and Confirm Payment

You'll see a confirmation screen showing "Pay $1.25" with all the payment details:

  • To: The recipient address (truncated for display)
  • BTC equivalent: Shows the amount in satoshis (e.g., "1,430 sats")
  • Exchange rate: Current Bitcoin price
  • Arrives: "Instantly"
  • Fees: "Free"
  • Total: $1.25

Review all details carefully, then click "Confirm and pay" to complete your payment. Remember: "All bitcoin sales and transfers are final."

5

Payment Successful!

After confirming, you'll see a success screen with a green checkmark.

The screen will display "You paid $1.25" confirming your payment was successful.

Tap "Done" to close the confirmation screen.

6

View Your Payment Receipt

You can view the payment receipt in your transaction history. Tap the clock icon at the bottom navigation to access your Activity feed.

Your payment will appear in your Activity feed, organized by date. You'll see entries like:

  • Bitcoin payment with "Cash balance" as the subtitle
  • The amount "$1.25" on the right
  • The time or date of the transaction
7

View Transaction Details (Optional)

You can view detailed information about your payment by tapping on the transaction in your Activity feed.

The transaction details screen shows:

  • Payment summary with amount in USD and satoshis
  • Exchange rate and fees breakdown
  • Transaction status (Complete)
  • Recipient address (with copy option)

Need help? Make sure you have:

  • CashApp installed and logged in
  • At least $1.25 in your Cash or Bitcoin balance
  • A stable internet connection
