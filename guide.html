How to Pay with CashApp

Follow these simple steps to pay the Lightning invoice using CashApp on your mobile device.

1

Open CashApp and Click the $ Icon

Open the CashApp on your mobile device. You'll see the main screen with a keypad showing "$0".

2

Scan the QR Code

Click the scan QR button in the top left corner to scan the QR code displayed on https://so-big-lightning-payment.vercel.app/

3

Select Cash as Your Funding Source

After scanning, CashApp will ask: "How do you want to fund your $1.25 bitcoin payment?"

Select Cash but you may see Bitcoin listed if you have any.

4

Review and Confirm Payment

You'll see a confirmation screen showing "Pay $1.25" with all the payment details.

5

Payment Successful!

After confirming, you'll see a success screen with a green checkmark.

The screen will display "You paid $1.25" confirming your payment was successful.

6

View Your Payment Receipt

You can view the payment receipt in your transaction history. Tap the clock icon at the bottom navigation to access your Activity feed.

7

View Transaction Details (Optional)

You can view detailed information about your payment by tapping on the transaction in your Activity feed.
